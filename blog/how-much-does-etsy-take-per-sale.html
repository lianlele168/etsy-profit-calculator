Etsy fees

How Much Does Etsy Take Per Sale?

Etsy fees usually combine a listing fee, transaction fee, payment processing fee, and optional advertising costs. The exact percentage depends on seller location, shipping, tax handling, and whether the order came from Offsite Ads.

Start with the order revenue

Use the item price plus shipping charged to the buyer, then subtract discounts. This is the main base for marketplace fees.

Include fixed and variable fees

Fixed fees hurt low-price products more. A small item can look profitable until the listing fee, processing flat fee, and shipping cost are added.
