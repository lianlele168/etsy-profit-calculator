Etsy Fees on Shipping: Do You Pay Fees on Shipping?

Yes, sellers should expect marketplace fees to interact with shipping charged to the buyer. The shipping amount is also different from your actual carrier or fulfillment cost.

Shipping charged vs shipping cost

Shipping charged is revenue from the buyer. Shipping cost is what you pay to fulfill the order. Your profit calculation needs both.

Free shipping is not free

If you absorb shipping without raising the item price, margin falls. If you roll shipping into the item price, buyer total can stay similar, but conversion may change.
