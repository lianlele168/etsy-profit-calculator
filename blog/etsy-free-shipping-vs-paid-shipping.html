Etsy Free Shipping vs Paid Shipping

Free shipping is a pricing decision, not a cost disappearing act. Sellers either absorb the cost or include it in the item price.

When free shipping can work

It can work when the item has enough margin, shipping cost is predictable, and the adjusted item price still feels acceptable to buyers.

When it hurts

It hurts when shipping cost varies widely, the item is low-priced, or Offsite Ads can be triggered on already thin margins.
