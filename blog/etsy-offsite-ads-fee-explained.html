Offsite Ads

Etsy Offsite Ads Fee Explained

Offsite Ads are charged only when an attributed ad click leads to an order. For many sellers this can add a 15% fee, while qualifying higher-volume shops can receive a 12% rate.

Why sellers need to model it

A product with a thin margin can become unprofitable when Offsite Ads are added on top of ordinary Etsy fees and fulfillment costs.

Use risk mode

Run every important product once with Offsite Ads off and once with Offsite Ads on. The second result is your margin stress test.
